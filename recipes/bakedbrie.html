Baked Brie

Baked Brie is a decadent dish wherein brie is wrapped in puff pastry and then baked. Alas, it is delicious as well and you will find it hard to stop eating, even as your body cries out for mercy.

Ingredients

  • Brie (8 ounces)
  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 egg white

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a baking sheet.
  2. Wrap the brie in a sheet of puff pastry and place on a baking sheet, seam down.
  3. Brush puff pastry with egg white.
  4. Bake until pastry is golden brownâ€”about 30 minutes. Serve immediately.
